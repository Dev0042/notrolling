About The Show

THE NO TROLLING
PODCAST

Born in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, the No Trolling Podcast is one of East Africa's most energetic and unfiltered conversation spaces. Hosted by Sean (@sean81jax), the show launched with a bold premise: real conversations with real people — no gatekeeping, no corporate polish, no trolling. Just honest talk, wild takes, and genuine connection.

From relationships and dating culture to entertainment, mental health, comedy, and career journeys — every episode dives deep. With 72+ episodes and a growing community of 68K+ followers, NT has become the go-to podcast for East African youth. Don't Sweat It — this is Advice For No Price.
